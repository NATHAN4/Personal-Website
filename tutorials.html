Android App Development
After many requests for Android help/private lessons, I'll be beginning my Android tutorial series in early January. This series will begin with the absolute basics of Android Studio and mobile development. As boring as the basics can be, it's important to build a sturdy base before climbing up to more advanced topics like 'Material' design, custom controls, asynchronous tasks and networking. Suggestions and requests can be sent to my email using the contact link in the header.

For the best learning outcome, it's recommended to follow this tutorial series fully in chronological order. If you already have Android Studio installed and configured, however, feel free to skip to Chapter 1.2.
